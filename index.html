About CodeFest 2023

CodeFest 2023 is a 24-hour coding marathon that brings together developers, designers, and innovators to create amazing solutions to real-world problems. This year's theme is "Building a Sustainable Future" where participants will work on projects related to environmental sustainability, social impact, and technological innovation.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, CodeFest offers a platform to learn, collaborate, and showcase your skills. With mentors from top tech companies, exciting workshops, and networking opportunities, this is an event you don't want to miss!

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in CodeFest 2023?
CodeFest 2023 is open to all college students, recent graduates, and young professionals. Participants can join individually or in teams of 2-4 members.
Is there a registration fee?
No, CodeFest 2023 is completely free to attend. We provide meals, snacks, and swag to all participants.
What should I bring to the event?
Participants should bring their laptops, chargers, and any other devices they need for development. We'll provide power strips, Wi-Fi, and workspace. Don't forget to bring a sleeping bag if you plan to stay overnight!
Will there be mentors available during the event?
Yes, we'll have mentors from various tech companies available throughout the event to help with technical challenges and provide guidance on projects.
